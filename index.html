about me

coding feels like creating worlds.

I’m Amy, 18, a programmer with big plans and an even bigger imagination. I create websites, write code, and I’m currently about to build my own game. For me, coding feels like creating worlds — turning ideas in my head into something people can actually see, click, and experience.

I want to get rich, not just for myself, but so I can give my mom and the people I love everything they’ve ever wanted. Some people doubt me. Some think I only talk and nothing will happen. Cute assumption, but wrong.

I don’t always explain what I’m doing, because not everything needs to be announced. I know what I’m building, and that’s enough.

Pinterest is one of my favorite places for visual inspiration. My interests shift constantly, but that’s part of me — I’m always becoming someone new.
